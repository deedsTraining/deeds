More Information:

Our courses are held at the University of Toronto, St. George campus (downtown Toronto)

Course topics, start dates, duration, and tuition fees are subject to change.

All of our courses are open to anyone. Our courses are not part of any academic program at the University of Toronto. Our courses are administered by the University of Toronto.

All courses have a maximum and a minimum enrollment. Courses are full when the maximum enrollment is reached. Courses will be cancelled or re-scheduled if the minimum enrollment is not reached by the start date of the course.

There are no lectures or computer labs or when the Robarts Library at the University of Toronto is closed.
