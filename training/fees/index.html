Fee Payments

Fee Payment Due Date:

Tuition Fees are payable in full by the start of the first session of the course.

Tuition Fees are not deferable. If the Tuition Fees are not paid then you must withdraw from the course.

Method of Payment:

At the present time we can accept only Certified Cheques or Money Drafts in the full amount.

To obtain a Certified Cheque or Money Order you must go to a bank.

Certified Cheques or Money Orders are payable to the University of Toronto.

Sponsorship:

If your tuition fees are being paid by your company or through Employment Ontario, we require verification from your employer or your counsellor that your tuition fees will be paid and when they will be paid.

Payment can be made with a Company cheque or by direct deposit. Contact us for address or account information.
