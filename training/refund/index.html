Refund Policy

Course Cancelled:

If a course is cancelled any tuition fees pre-paid will be refunded.

Cancellations will always be communicated in writing as soon as possible before the actual start of a course.

Course Delayed or Re-scheduled:

If a course start date is changed any tuition fees pre-paid will be refunded. Optionally, pre-paid tuition fees can be used for payment on the re-scheduled course.

Delay of, or re-scheduling of, a course will always be communicated in writing as soon as possible before the actual start of a course.

Withdrawal:

For courses of 10 sessions or less, a full refund will be made, if you withdraw from the course before the beginning of the second session of the course. Your withdrawal must be communicated in writing. Any materials provided must be returned in "new" condition or a penalty will be assessed.

For courses of 10 sessions or less, if you withdraw from the course at a later time, refunds will be pro-rated. Course materials provided cannot be returned for credit. Any fees paid on your behalf are not refundable.

For courses of longer duration, a full refund will be made, if you withdraw from the course before the third session of the course. Your withdrawal must be communicated in writing. Any materials provided must be returned in "new" condition or a penalty will be assessed.

If you withdraw from a course which is longer than 10 sessions after the third session, refunds will be pro-rated. Course materials provided cannot be returned for credit. Any fees paid on your behalf are not refundable.
